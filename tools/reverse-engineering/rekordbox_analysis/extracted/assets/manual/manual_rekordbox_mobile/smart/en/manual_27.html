Glossary

BEAT GRID

The beats detected when music files are analyzed are displayed as a grid of dots or lines on the enlarged waveform display.

CUE/LOOP POINT MEMORY

Cue and loop points can be set and saved ahead of time then called out on DJ players. With rekordbox, up to 10 cue and loop points can be saved per music file.

HISTORY

The history of tracks played on DJ players can be fed back to rekordbox. Playing histories can be saved in rekordbox as playlists.

HOT CUE

By storing cue and loop points as hot cues, the points can be cued and regular or loop playback can be started at the touch of a button on DJ players. With rekordbox, up to three hot cues can be stored per music file. (* For XDJ-RX, up to four hot cues can be stored.)

LINK EXPORT

When a DJ player and computer (or mobile device) are connected by LAN cable, wireless LAN or USB cable, rekordbox music files and data can be loaded in real time on the DJ player.

PLAYLIST

Playlists can be created to organize your rekordbox music collection according to your DJ performance requirements. By creating playlists of your favorite music files ahead of time, the desired music files can be selected quickly on DJ players.

PRO DJ LINK

By interconnecting Pioneer DJ units supporting PRO DJ LINK by LAN cable, rekordbox music files can be shared with a DJ mixer and multiple DJ players. Up to 4 DJ players can participate in a network. (* For XDJ-AERO and XDJ-RX, only one DJ PLAYER can participate in the network. XDJ-AERO and XDJ-RX cannot participate in the same network as other DJ PLAYERs.) If the network has wireless LAN access points, up to 4 mobile devices on which rekordbox (iOS/Android) is installed can also participate in the network, allowing smooth switching between DJ performers.

QUANTIZE

Cues, loops and hot cues are set to the nearest beat. By using rekordbox to detect the positions of beats in music files and adjusting the beat grids ahead of time, cues, loops and effects can be set and played on beat, even when the DJ unit’s buttons are operated roughly.

TAG LIST

Tracks selected according to specific DJ situations can be marked and added to taglists. The taglists can be shared online among multiple DJ players connected on the LAN. By operating the respective DJ players, the music files on the taglists can be loaded into and played on the respective DJ players, and taglists actually used during performances can be saved as rekordbox playlists.

  • For instructions on accessing taglist from DJ players, see the operating instructions for the respective DJ players.

Linking with a Computer

The mobile device can be connected by wireless LAN to a computer on which rekordbox (Mac/Windows) is installed to transfer music data between it and rekordbox (Mac/Windows).

Depending on the settings of the security software, the operating system, etc., being used on the computer, communications between the computer and mobile device may be interrupted. For details about the communications environment required on the computer (programs, operating system, network, etc.), see the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) operating instructions.

1Connect the computer on which rekordbox (Mac/Windows) is installed to the wireless LAN network.

  • For instructions on making the wireless LAN connections, see the instructions of your computer or wireless LAN device.

2Connect the mobile device on which rekordbox (iOS/Android) is installed to the wireless LAN network.

  • For instructions on making the wireless LAN connections, see the instructions of your mobile device and wireless LAN device.

3Launch rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

  • For operations on the computer, see the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) operating instructions.

4On rekordbox (iOS/Android)’s top menu, tap [Connect].

rekordbox (iOS/Android) stands by for the linking operation (mobile device search) to be performed on rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

5Open the [Device] pane in rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer and click at the bottom left of the [Device] pane.

  • For operations on the computer, see the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) operating instructions.

When the link is established, the mobile device’s name is displayed on the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) [Device] pane.

Notes

Only one mobile device can be linked at a time to rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

The name of the mobile device displayed on the [Device] pane can be checked and changed using rekordbox (iOS/Android).

  • Preferences

Connecting to the computer with a USB cable

Android devices that support the MSC mode operate as USB storage devices (secondary storage devices such as USB flash memory drives) when connected to the computer by USB cable.

Connect the Android device set to the MSC mode to the computer using a USB cable, then launch rekordbox (Mac/Windows). The name of the mobile device (Android device) is displayed on the [Device] pane in rekordbox (Mac/Windows).

  • For instructions on setting the MSC mode, see your Android device’s instructions. If the computer does not recognize your Android device as a USB storage device (a secondary storage such as a USB flash memory drive), check with the manufacturer of your Android device or your store of purchase.

Canceling the link with the computer

Open the [Device] pane in rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer and click at the right of the device icon to cancel the link.

Sending Updated Info to a Computer

For music files and playlists imported from rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer to rekordbox (iOS/Android), the information updated using rekordbox (iOS/Android) can be updated in rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

Establish a link between the mobile device and the computer before reflecting the information that has been updated using rekordbox (iOS/Android) in rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

  • Linking with a Computer

1Open the [Device] pane in rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

  • For operations on the computer, see the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) operating instructions.

2Right-click the mobile device’s icon displayed at the top of the [Device] pane.

  • For operations on the computer, see the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) operating instructions.

  • The name of the mobile device displayed on the [Device] pane can be checked and changed using rekordbox (iOS/Android).

    • Preferences

3Click [Update Collection].

  • To update one track at a time, select the music file, right-click, then click [Update Collection].

The following items are updated in the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) library:

  • Cue/loop point memories

  • Hot cues

  • Beat grid

  • Track information (Rating, Color, Comments)

Notes

To export a playlist from the mobile device, drag the playlist from the [Device] pane to the [Playlists] pane.

To cancel the link with the computer, click at the right of the device icon on the [Device] pane in rekordbox (Mac/Windows).

Exporting the “My Settings” values

It is also possible to export the “My Settings” values stored in rekordbox (iOS/Android) to rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

1Open the [Device] pane in rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.

  • For operations on the computer, see the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) operating instructions.

2Right-click the icon of the mobile device displayed on the [Device] pane.

  • For operations on the computer, see the rekordbox (Mac/Windows) operating instructions.

  • The name of the mobile device displayed on the [Device] pane can be checked and changed using rekordbox (iOS/Android).

    • Preferences

3Click [Import My Settings].

  • The “My Settings” values stored in rekordbox (iOS/Android) are reflected in rekordbox (Mac/Windows) on the computer.
